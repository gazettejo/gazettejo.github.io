WORDS FROM THE AUTHOR

I started writing this book at the age of 15. Throughout my childhood, publishing a book had always been a precious dream of mine. La Gazette de Jo is a book that I wrote during my free times for over two years. My little dream is finally coming true! It is with pleasure today that I share with you, dear reader, my creation.
